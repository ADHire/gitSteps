Swap back over to your terminal, and paste what you had copied. Don't worry about pasting twice, your terminal/command prompt will automatically run the first command that has been pasted, then it will require you to press ENTER to submit the second command.

Once you press ENTER to submit that second line, you may or may not be prompted for your GitHub username/password. Please remember that when prompted for your password, it may seem like you aren't typing because there will be no input shown. This is a security check so others cannot see your password as it is being typed in.

Once you submit your username and password, you should be seeing a bunch of output from your terminal. It will be a long list containing things such as: 'Counting Objects', 'Delta Compression', 'Writing Objects', and some other cool things.
